🌙 About Okinawa Halal Kitchen

Okinawa’s First 100% Halal Fast Food Kitchen

Okinawa Halal Kitchen is a new 100% halal fast food kitchen opening in Nakagusuku, Okinawa. We serve fresh, clean and delicious halal food for the Muslim community, local families, tourists and everyone visiting Okinawa.

Located about 3 km from Okinawa Mosque, we proudly offer halal burgers, fried chicken, chicken rolls, chicken strips, biryani and many special menu items prepared with care.

Our flavor is carefully balanced with international taste standards, so people from different countries and cultures can enjoy it easily. This special taste is available only at Okinawa Halal Kitchen.
